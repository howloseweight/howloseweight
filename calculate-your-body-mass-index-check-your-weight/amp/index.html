Calculate Your Body Mass Index – Check Your Weight

Calculate your body mass index, or BMI, to figure out whether you should lose weight. Body BMI is used to assess the adequacy of a person’s height and weight to determine if a person is underweight, overweight.

BMI is very simple to calculate. You will need a pen, a piece of paper and a calculator.

Calculate the BMI will help us the formula:

BMI = weight in kilograms / height in meters squared

How accurate is the BMI mass?

Calculating BMI gives you a very clear idea of ​​whether you should lose or gain weight. With a BMI of 23 for women and 26 for men, nutritionists advise thinking about losing weight.

However, experts warn that the BMI index is an average indicator and is not intended for in-depth diagnosis of the body. In other words, a BMI calculation gives an average result.

In the practice of doctors, there are frequent cases where the BMI of a woman with severe flaccidity of the skin and fat deposits in the abdominal area is within the normal range. Those. nothing threatens the patient’s health, but she is unhappy with her figure and wants to lose weight. And, on the contrary, when examining an athlete with a pronounced musculature, body weight can be large for her height. BMI, respectively, too. In this case, the girl will have great quality body and developed muscles. Therefore, in order not to be mistaken about this, a whole series of tests will be required – just calculating the BMI is not enough.

However, if the BMI speaks of severe obesity, the error is almost eliminated – you need to think about health. At least visit a doctor. And what is your BMI?
