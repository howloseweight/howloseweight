Water With Turmeric Recipe

There are at least 3 reasons to take water with turmeric every morning. Water with turmeric has an amazing effect on the body – on the skin, metabolic processes, the state of the digestive tract.

  • Water with turmeric is good for digestion, stimulates the processes of food digestion. Turmeric warms from the inside and generates a digestive fire that helps digest even high-calorie meals. In the period of feasts and holidays such water is simply necessary.
  • Water with lemon and turmeric cleans not only from the inside, but also from the outside. It has a beneficial effect on the skin. Your face will look more rested and healthy, without pimples and redness.
  • And, of course, fat burning. Turmeric helps speed up metabolism, remove toxins and toxins.
    • Turmeric with water: a recipe

    There are two ways to make this wonderful drink. The first is to simply dilute the seasoning in warm water. The second option is complicated. To make a drink, you need to cool the boiled water, add the juice of half a lemon, a teaspoon of honey and a half teaspoon of turmeric. Mix well, give a little brew. Warm water with turmeric, honey and lemon is ready!

    Warm Water With Turmeric

    You can also add a little ginger. But this is optional.

    How to drink turmeric water?

    Turmeric is drunk with water on an empty stomach – 30 minutes before meals. You can prepare it after waking up and immediately drink.
