Slimming cocktail recipe

Celery slimming cocktail is extremely simple: prepare all the ingredients, wash, peel and chop in a blender. To do this, you will need: an average bunch of celery, 50 ml of natural nonfat yogurt, 1 lime. The cocktail will be airy, soft and very fragrant. It is better to drink an hour before meals at any time of the day or at night, so as not to suffer from hungry insomnia. By analogy, you can make a kefir-celery cocktail (there is no big difference what kind of fermented milk product you take).
