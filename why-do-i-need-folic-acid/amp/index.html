Why Do I Need Folic Acid?

The world seems gray and impersonal? Do you want to spend the whole day under a blanket with a book? Does the community of colleagues and friends upset and annoying you? You feel depressed and distracted? There may be a shortage of folic acid. But this can be corrected.

Folic acid is responsible for producing happiness, and it is also very important for women. It participates in the metabolism of amino acids, the synthesis of nucleic acids, is important in the formation of new cells, blood formation. Folic acid is also needed to support the immune and cardiac systems.

  • Daily intake of folic acid – 140 – 144 µg.
