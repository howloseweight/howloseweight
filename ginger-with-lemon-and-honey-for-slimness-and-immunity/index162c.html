Ginger with lemon and honey: a recipe

For the preparation of the elixir of health, slimness and beauty, we need honey, ginger, lemon (proportions: 250 g / 300 g / 200 g, respectively).

Ginger With Lemon And Almond

The juicier the ginger root, the more liquid the mixture will be.

Peel the ginger root and cut into medium sized pieces. Pour boiling water over the lemon, then crumble it into slices and remove the bones. Put everything in the bowl of the blender and chop it thoroughly (you can take the meat grinder). Use lemon with the skin.

Honey is added to the obtained homogeneous mixture of ginger and lemon, in a specified amount – 250 g. Mix well, put in a dry glass dish with a lid, put in the fridge for a day. After 24 hours, the product is completely ready for consumption – you can eat with spoons or add it to tea, fruit drinks, herbal infusion and other beverages.

Store the product predominantly in the refrigerator, under the lid, in a glass container. Try to use the mixture for a month, then it is better to prepare a fresh drug (do not store it, it is better to do it more often, but fresh).

How to take?

It is recommended to use tea with ginger, lemon and honey. This drink is the easiest to digest. The warming effect and excellent taste have made this hot cocktail the most popular for colds.

Add a little spoonful of the mixture in a slightly cooled tea (not in boiling water – very few useful substances will remain). For those who like the taste of the mixture, we recommend taking it in its pure form. You can also drink tea or boiled water at room temperature.

Drink ginger – lemon – honey is not recommended for individual intolerance to the components of the mixture, as well as for pregnant and lactating women, young children.
