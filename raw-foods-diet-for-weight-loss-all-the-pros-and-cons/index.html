  • the presence of gastrointestinal diseases (acids in fresh foods irritate the gastric mucosa);

    Going to raw foods: how to start?

    Raw food for beginners is recommended to take smoothly.

    A smooth transition to raw foods is recommended to start like this:

  • In the first week, refuse semi-finished products and sausages, in the second week remove red meat, in the third – white, in the fourth – eggs, in the fifth – fish, in the sixth – milk and fermented milk products, in the seventh – cereals (raw foodists do not cook cereals) ;
  • every week, every day, gradually increase the share of raw food in the diet.

    • The most “convenient” periods for the beginning are autumn and summer, at this time the most fresh and inexpensive products. In addition, when the sun is shining around and the birds are singing, it is much easier to switch to a fruit and vegetable diet – in the winter, rejection of high-calorie foods is harder.
