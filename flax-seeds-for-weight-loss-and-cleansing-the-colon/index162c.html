    Flax Seeds Are Good For Bowel

  • 1 – decoction of flax seed. To prepare, take a tablespoon of seeds and pour boiling water in a volume of 0.5 liters. After the container must be put on a small fire and cook the decoction under the lid for one and a half – two hours, stirring occasionally. Take 10 days in a row, three times a day, 30 minutes before meals.
  • 2 – infusion of flax seed. Also, a tablespoon of seed, poured with two cups of boiling water, is taken and infused overnight (always in a warm place). Next morning the medicine is ready. Take 100 ml 2 – 3 times a day. It is advisable to drink the fresh (if it stays for a long time, it will get an unpleasant taste).
  • 3 – flax seed for weight loss with kefir. For all fans of dairy products – the best solution. We take 100 grams of kefir and add seeds in the following order: 1 tsp (in the first week), 2 in the second, 3 in the third, respectively.

    Flax: good and harm

    Is it possible to harm yourself by taking flax seeds?

    Here it is important to understand what, how and for what we do.

  • First, do not forget that the rapid discharge of fat mass – stress for the body, it is harmful, is fraught with consequences. Therefore, we do everything without fanaticism. Thoughtfully and consciously.
  • Secondly, effective weight loss is always a system. Try to keep track of your diet, avoid fried and fatty, sweet, smoked, sausage and bakery products. Take flax seed should be systematically (at least 1 – 3 weeks, after which a break is taken).
  • Thirdly, we observe the norm, not more than 1 tablespoon per day. Even if you eat a bag of flax, lose weight by morning will not succeed. Seeds for weight loss “work” only in the complex.
  • And finally, remember about contraindications and do not ignore visits to the doctor. If you have any health problems, do not start the reception without consulting a doctor. He will examine you and provide you with a complete list of contraindications. Here is part of the list: pregnancy, hepatitis, cirrhosis of the liver, inflammation of the cornea of ​​the eye, acute intestinal disorders. In diabetes, flax is allowed with caution under the supervision of a doctor (in a smaller amount and only in the form of decoctions).

    • As you can see, cleaning with flax seeds has practically no contraindications. The main thing is not to exceed the recommended rate and drink plenty of fluids during the reception. To prevent the product from spoiling, keep it in a dark, cool place, preferably in a glass or ceramic dish with the lid tightly closed. Treatment with flax seeds gives the result in just a couple of weeks. During this period, you completely rid your body of edema, accelerate metabolic and recovery processes.

    Cleaning flax seeds helps reduce cravings for sweets and other hazards. But do not forget, flax for weight loss is not a panacea and not a magic wand. A miracle will not happen if you eat a fat kebab or a cake with flax seeds.
