With explicit overweight, excessive unhealthy appetite, or if you can not help the teenagers to lose weight fast on their own, you should consult your doctor. It will produce inspection, weigh teenager, rate his overall health. He will say, whether you need to worry, and make recommendations how to normalize and maintain a healthy weight: he will recommend diet and exercise program.

Next you need to implement all of these recommendations, instill love for a healthy lifestyle for teenager. You need to act together and gradually, without strict prohibitions and orders in order to new habits have become part of everyday life. Pay attention to diet and lifestyle of the whole family. How often do you exercise and just go for a walk, if you eat at night, often on the table if there are sweets, cakes and other unhealthy high-calorie meals? You should go with a teenager in the supermarket and choose healthy foods that are loved by all. Then choose kind of activity to be able to train together or enroll teenager in the sports section. Teenager lose weight faster if he will like what he eats and what he does. Proper nutrition and physical activity should not be seen as a punishment or heavy duty.

Teenager can lose weight fast only by changing diet and increasing physical activity. Medication ways to lose weight fast, various unbalanced diets, use of teas or diet pills are unacceptable. The only exceptions are cases where the decision was made by the doctor about taking drugs.

How should eat teenagers, to lose weight fast?

Sandwich of healthy food

Modern teenagers eat basically randomly, giving preference to food that can be eaten on the go and has a bright taste. Such a food does not contain essential nutrients. It is very little protein, fiber, vitamins in this products, but a lot of that from which spoiled teeth, increased blood sugar levels and increased weight. Explain teenager that food should be a source of energy or health. Own example will be very convincing. Just stop to buy such food. Take dried fruits for dessert, crispbread instead of potato chips, chocolate bars replace with muesli (without sugar, chocolate and other sweet and unnatural additives). Invent recipes of “safe” sandwiches from the bran bread, low-fat cheese or cottage cheese, boiled chicken, sliced cucumbers or tomatoes, lettuce. Use yogurt-based sauce or, in extreme cases, low-fat sour cream as a filling of salad.

In this age of increased protein needs, it must be obtained from lean meat, chicken, fish, cottage cheese, cheese, milk, cereals and corn bread.

In order to losing weight is not caused protest and held smoothly and painlessly, divide the process into stages.
