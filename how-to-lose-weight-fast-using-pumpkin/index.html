Pumpkin Recipes for Quick Weight Loss

pumpkin for cooking

In order to lose weight was not only useful, but also tasty pumpkin dishes should not be monotonous. There are plenty of recipes from this vegetable. Here are some of these recipes:

Pumpkin porridge
Purified pumpkin cut into small pieces.
Simmer in a small amount of water until half cooked (25 – 30 min.)
Add 3 tablespoons favorite cereals and cook until tender.
At the end of cooking if desired, you can add nuts, dried apricots or raisins.
Finely chop on a kitchen grater.

Pumpkin salad with fruit or vegetables
Pumpkin and fruit (sweet apple, pear, apricot) or vegetables (carrots, beets, cucumber) finely chop on a kitchen grater
Salad fill nonfat yogurt or fresh squeezed lemon juice

Pumpkin stew with vegetables for fast weight loss
Necessary products – pumpkin, 3 carrots, 4 tomatoes, parsley, cabbage, lettuce, canned beans bank.
Necessary to clean the pumpkin and carrots, cut into small cubes and fry in a small amount of olive oil.
Next, pour a little water and simmer.
Add beans and tomatoes for 5-10 minutes until cooked
Before use, sprinkle with chopped lettuce, parsley.
