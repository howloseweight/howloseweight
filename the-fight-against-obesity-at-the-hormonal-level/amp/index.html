The Fight Against Obesity At The Hormonal Level

According to nutritionists, the primary cause of obesity is not overeating, but hormonal imbalance. A person is dramatically getting fat due to inhibition of metabolism and metabolic disorders. That is why many standard diets do not help or give a short-term effect. To get rid of extra pounds once and for all, you need a special metabolic diet, a detailed description of which is presented in the article.

Efficiency and indications

Metabolic diet to combat excess weight is a special nutritional system aimed at correcting hormonal balance. It reduces the synthesis of hormones that contribute to the deposition of fat, and activates the production of the opposite – fat burning. Such a diet will help to lose up to 15 kilograms in thirty days without any harm and physical exertion.

It is shown proper nutrition for people with metabolic clinical syndrome. This is a complex of disorders of the internal organs, which can lead not only to obesity, but also to diabetes, as well as to cardiovascular diseases. You can suspect the presence of the syndrome on the basis of characteristic features:

    • Diet with metabolic syndrome increases the level of hormones that stimulate the metabolism: adrenaline and testosterone. Ordinary products that are accessible to everyone, which you just need to competently combine, will help to do this.

    Stages and proper nutrition scheme

    To find the perfect figure, you have to go through three mandatory steps. The menu of the metabolic diet is planned taking into account the calorie content of food, which is calculated by points from zero to four. During its observance, it is recommended to drink at least one liter of ordinary water per day, and also adhere to the rules:

  • Bake instead of frying.
  • Reduce salt intake.
  • Refuse sugar.
  • Eliminate easily digestible carbohydrates.
  • Eat slowly, enjoying every bite.

    • Metabolic diet stage 1 – preparing the body for weight loss. For two weeks it is necessary to eat only foods with a calorie content of 0 points. If there is a weakness, you can dilute the menu with sweet tea or compote with honey.
