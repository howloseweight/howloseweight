LPG Treatment Helps To Lose Weight – True Or Myth

If you are interested in beauty treatments, you probably heard about LPG-massage. Advertising describes it as a unique and extremely effective technology that will help you quickly and easily cope with excess weight, cellulite and age-related skin problems. But is it worth believing advertising? Let’s try to figure it out.

LPG massage what it is: more about the technology

Lpg-massage – a kind of apparatus massage, developed in the 70s of the last century by French explorer Louis Paul Gitel. Surface treatment of the patient’s body is carried out with special nozzles, which, with a high frequency, capture the skin areas, massage them and apply vacuum, stretching fat deposits and activating blood circulation.

The promised effect of the course of the procedure is to reduce the weight and reduce the “orange peel”, improve the overall skin tone, correct the contours of the body and face, reduce the severity of wrinkles and other age-related changes.

Lpg Massage Machine

Indications for LPG massage:
  • Obesity;
  • Flabbiness of the skin;
  • Wrinkles of various etiologies;
  • Stretch marks and scars, including after childbirth and injuries;
  • Cellulite;
  • swelling of tissues;
  • skin sagging against the background of a sharp weight loss;
  • Earthy and dull skin tone.

    • Vacuum massage lpg is also recommended as a preventive measure for athletes and other people with high physical activity.

    lpg massage contraindications and restrictions

    Lpg Massage

    LPG massage can not be done in the following cases:
  • Pregnancy and breastfeeding period;
  • Non-healing skin wounds;
  • Rash, ulcers, scratches and similar dermatological problems;
  • Infection of the skin;
  • Oncological diseases;
  • Period of menstruation;
  • Highly elevated body temperature;
  • Epilepsy;
  • Inflammation of the lymph nodes;
  • Chronic diseases in the period of exacerbation.

    • Lpg Massage Review

    If you have problems with the cardiovascular system, you should consult with your doctor before starting the course whether lpg massage is possible in your case.
    Dignity and performance

    One of the main advantages of LPG massage is the fact that it is performed by hardware. Unlike a person, the machine does not get tired and is able to work out the entire session equally effectively. Also on modern equipment it is possible to very flexibly adjust the settings and select the most appropriate mode of exposure to the skin for each patient and each area of ​​his body. The optimal program LPG-massage will be able to pick up each person, regardless of his age and the existing problems with health and overweight. With proper equipment setup, the procedure is completely safe – there will not even be bruises left.

    Lpg Massage Before And After

    As for the effectiveness of LPG massage, it is not at all as great as the advertising promises. Almost everyone notes the overall positive effect of the procedure on the body and the improvement of skin tone; for a number of people, it helped to reduce the expression of stretch marks, tighten the contours of the body, reduce wrinkles.

    However, no one can boast a significant weight loss from massage alone. Weight loss may be absent altogether or be insignificant and is associated only with a decrease in edema and more active blood circulation. Serious weight reduction is possible only with the complex effects of massage, sports activities and the right diet.

    Summary

    LPG massage is definitely a useful and effective procedure. However, do not overestimate its capabilities and expect miracles. The course of such a massage, like any other, will help you tighten the skin and keep the body in good shape. But to seriously lose weight and completely part with the hated cellulite, you have to work hard, some hardware procedures are not enough.
