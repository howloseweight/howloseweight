Homemade Blueberry Smoothies: Recipe

Blueberry Smoothie

To make a detox smoothie, we need:
  • 2 cups blueberries;
  • 1 glass of natural yogurt;
  • 1 cup of coconut milk;
  • 1 frozen banana.

    • Divide the ingredients in half. Mix the first half in a blender and send in the freezer for an hour. Then mix the second part of all ingredients and mix both parts. A refreshing, hearty and flavorful blueberry smoothie is ready!
