Paleo Diet

Fresh juice of beets, carrots, celery, grapefruit and lemon.

The Paleo Diet is loved by athletes, sportsmen, professional fitness trainers, tennis players, models, and almost half the cast of Hollywood. The secret of popularity is quite simple: Paleo diet allows us to eat your fill, and literally forced to watch what goes into your mouth, but does not require counting calories. Also on this diet, you can learn how to cook delicious dishes of meat and vegetables, how to eat nuts and not get fat. This diet plan is aimed against the limitation of healthy fats, and does not require the complete abandonment of carbohydrates, similar as the Atkins diet, or permanent tracking ratio of fat, carbohydrate and protein calories diet as the Zone Diet.

At the same time, the Paleo diet helps to lose weight and achieve impressive results in any sport, as it contains everything you need for recovery and muscle growth. And she has no “of withdrawal syndrome”, as it is not a low-calorie diet and does not slows metabolism.

Paleo diet menu

Paleo diet. Fried fish over a campfire

However, you need a little practice and you will be able to make Paleo ration for every taste and wallet.

Here are approximate the Paleo diet menu for a woman weighing 130 pounds.

  • Breakfast: a mixture of 2 ounces of nuts and sliced apples or pears.
  • Snack: 200 pounds of berries, 4 ounces of chicken breast
  • Lunch: a portion of wheat germ, carrot salad with apple and beetroot (raw vegetables, lemon juice, a little olive oil), a portion of veal or other lean red meat.
  • Snack: any fruit
  • Dinner: salad of green leafy vegetables, tomatoes, cucumbers and cabbage, a portion of red or white fish cooked on the grill.
